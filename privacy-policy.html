Privacy Policy for Golden Coins Puzzles

Effective Date:11 November 2025

Introduction

Golden Coins Puzzles (“the Game”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game. We keep things simple: we do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or login details.

Information We Collect

  • No Accounts: The Game does not require you to sign up, log in, or provide personal information.
  • Unity Ads: The Game uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect certain non‑personal data (such as device type, language, or general location) to provide relevant ads. This data is managed by Unity Technologies according to their privacy practices.

How We Use Information

  • We do not store or use personal information.
  • Any data collected by Unity Ads is used solely for advertising purposes and is managed by Unity Technologies.

Third‑Party Services

The Game uses Unity Ads. You can learn more about Unity’s privacy practices here: Unity Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents concerned about Unity Ads data collection should review Unity’s privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy
