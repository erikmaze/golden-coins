Introduction

Golden Coins Puzzles (“the Game”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game. We keep things simple: we do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or login details.

Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect personal information. However, our advertising partner Unity Ads may collect the following data:

  • Identifiers: Device identifiers (such as Apple’s IDFA or Android’s AAID) used for ad attribution and tracking.
  • Usage Data: Product interaction data (ad views, clicks, impressions) used to measure and improve ad performance.
  • Diagnostics: Crash and performance data to ensure ads function properly.
  • Location: Approximate location (such as country or region) to provide relevant ads.

Tracking

Unity Ads may use device identifiers and product interaction data for tracking purposes, meaning they may link this data with information collected from other apps and websites to deliver personalized advertisements and measure effectiveness.

How We Use Information

  • We do not store or use personal information directly.
  • Any data collected is handled by Unity Ads and used for advertising, analytics, and app functionality.

Third‑Party Services

The Game uses Unity Ads. You can learn more about Unity’s privacy practices here: Unity Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents concerned about Unity Ads data collection should review Unity’s privacy policy.
